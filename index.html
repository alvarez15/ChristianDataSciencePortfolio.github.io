Python TextBlob: A Sentiment Analysis Case Study

In this project, I conducted sentiment analysis using Python's TextBlob library to analyze the polarity of comments or text data. By applying TextBlob's natural language processing capabilities, I determined whether the comments were positive or negative in sentiment. Leveraging this sentiment classification, I then created word clouds to visually represent the most frequently occurring words in positive and negative comments separately. This approach allowed me to gain valuable insights into the prevailing sentiments expressed in the text data and identify prominent themes associated with positive and negative feedback. The word clouds offer an intuitive and compelling way to comprehend the overall sentiment and the key factors influencing the sentiments in the analyzed text corpus.
